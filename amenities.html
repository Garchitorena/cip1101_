Amenities

Luxurious, affordable accommodations without compromise. Located in Long Beach Bolinao, Pangasinan, Sunny Skies Beach Resort is famous for its long beach. Sunny Skies Beach Resort provides guests sensible practicality featuring modern aesthetics and amenities.

Resort Features and Facilities

Modern Guest Rooms

With 273 guest rooms with an area of 36 square-meters, Sunny Skies Beach Resort accommodations offer guests the best views of the beach on the otherside and its landscaped garden on the other.

Beach Cafe

Dine at the resort's Beach Cafe and experience the fine cuisine of Asian and Western influences beside the beautiful view of the beach.

Massive Swimming Pools

Sunny Skies Beach Resort offers 4 massive swimming pools. The resort has two full-size pools while the other two is located at the premier room.

Gym and Fitness Center

Keep fit during your stay in our resort in our well-equipped gym and fitness center. Our trained gym attendant is on hand to guide guests on machine usage and help them during their workouts.

Daily Housekeeping Service

Every morning, leaving the room to enjoy your stay in our resort, our daily housekeeping service will always make your rooms tidy and clean so when you return in your room, you can continue your relaxation after a long day of enjoyment.

Mini Shop

If you are in need of something that you left at home, you can stop by our mini shop where you can find all the essential things that you need.
